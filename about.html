Bio

The Barbarossa Brothers were originally 16th-century pirates, and as such we want this culinary experience to be exotic and adventurous. Wherever a boat can take you, we intend to accomplish that with our dishes. At the Barbarossa Brothers, you can have appetizers inspired by the flavors of the Mediterranean or a sandwich from the coast of Argentina, and a dessert served in Vietnamese holidays. The dishes at Barbarossa Brothers are globally inspired. Yet, we use a Cajun twist from over 10 years of Southern cooking experience. Our interior is quite youthful and colorful, a bit cabana, and hopefully chic. Everything from the food, website, to the art on the wall, is played part by millennials and we hope its a testament to our community.
